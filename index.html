The RLX needle was created with therapists in mind, wanting to make the process of electrolysis easier on you.

The right-angled needle allows you to sit behind the client when working on their face (as opposed to sitting at the side of the couch), giving you access to the face, neck & decollete without having to move position. This is a far more comfortable working position on your back, neck, shoulder, hands & wrists, allowing you to work for a longer duration due if required. Visibility of the hairs & hair follicle opening also becomes much clearer when sitting over a client. Areas of the body which can be difficult to reach become easier due to the angle of the needle.

The needle has a finely polished tapered end which makes it easier for you to insert into the hair follicle. The patented unsulated coating ensures the heat is directed to the exact place it is required. By concentrating where the heat is released, enables you to use less heat to treat each hair, providing your probing is accurate. The coating protects the skin surface from over treatment, lowering your clients discomfort and enhances your results especially if you are using the micro flash technique.

RLX needles are made in the UK from the finiest quality, medical grade stainless steel. Individually packaged & sterilsed (gamma irradiation).
